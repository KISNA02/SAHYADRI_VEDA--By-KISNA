Anjineri Hill is believed to be the birthplace of Lord Hanuman and holds great mythological importance. It is located about 20 km from Nashik, near Trimbakeshwar, and is a perfect blend of spirituality and adventure.

The trek begins with well-constructed steps and eventually leads to scenic plateaus, ancient caves, and temples. It is an easy trek suitable for all ages. During monsoon, the entire hill is lush green with misty views.
